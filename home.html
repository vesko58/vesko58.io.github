Toronto

The Toronto Christmas Market, which is held in the historic Distillery District, offers all the charm you will find in a European Christmas market with a few Canadian twists.

Vancouver

Located in downtown Vancouver, the Vancouver Christmas Market brings European holiday traditions to Canada's west coast. This market features lots of wooden stalls selling gifts, seasonal treats like bratwurst and mulled wine, and a beautiful Christmas carousel.
